RETAIN Challenge
SAS RETAIN persists a variable's value across rows , no direct pandas equivalent. Mapped to groupby().cumsum() or explicit loop with state.
FIRST./LAST. Challenge
Implicit boundary flags in SAS BY-group processing. In pandas: groupby().first() / .last() or shift()-based detection.
BY-group Processing
SAS processes data row-by-row with implicit sort contract. pandas is vectorised , the translation must preserve the sequential semantics.
